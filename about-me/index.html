About Me

Hi, I'm Tina.

I'm a qualified ski instructor and mum of two litter skiers. Originally from Scandinavia, I now split my time between Australia and Austria where my family is lucky enough to get 100+ days on the snow every year.

My little snow monkeys have been skiing since they could walk and over the years, I've learnt exactly what works and what doesn't when it comes to skiing with kids.

Whenever I'm trying to learn or do something new, I've always been greatful for bloggers who've shared their experiences with the world. And now that I know a thing or two about skiing with young kids, I thought I should do the same.

So subscribe below for regular tips and tricks that will make ski holidays with your family the joy it's supposed to be rather than the all-too-frequent exercise in expensive frustration it often is!
